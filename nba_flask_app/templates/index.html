NBA Analytics Project



For my final project, I wanted to know what stats affect a basketball team's ability to win or lose a game. Based upon my initial research, I found that Dean Oliver identified "Four Factors of Basketball Success". He based this on shooting(40%), turnovers(25%), rebounding(20%) and free throws(15%). The NBA measures this using the stats eFG%, TOV%, OREB%, and FTRate respectively. These factors can be expanded to the opposing team as well which is what the "fourfactor" model uses for it's model predictor features.

I wanted to improve upon this model so I decided to use some of the four factors with additional parameters. Recently, pace of play has become an emphasis in the NBA. When I tried to use this in my, "mymodel" predictor, I found that the pace had little impact (low coefficient value) so I ended up creating my own custom features to improve my prediction. I decided to use offensive and defensive ratings. I first calculated the average offensive and defensive ratings for teams in the past NBA season when they won a game. Using this value, I created a new feature for offensive and defensive effectiveness. This is seen in my "My Model" coefficient value of "OffEff" and "DefEff". I also used TS% instead of eFG% which takes into account three point field goal percentage. Based on these changes I was able to improve my model score from 0.9398(Four Factor Model) to 0.9536(My Model).
