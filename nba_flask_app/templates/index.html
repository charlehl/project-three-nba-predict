NBA Analytics Project

Game 4 of the 1987 NBA Finals: Magic Johnson "Junior Skyhook"



For my final project, I wanted to know what stats affect a basketball team's ability to win or lose a game. Based upon my initial research, I found that Dean Oliver identified "Four Factors of Basketball Success". He based this on shooting(40%), turnovers(25%), rebounding(20%) and free throws(15%). The NBA measures this using the stats eFG%, TOV%, OREB%, and FTRate respectively. These factors can be expanded to the opposing team as well to give a more accurate prediction which is what the "fourfactor" model uses for it's model predictor features.

Although my initial model was incredibly accurate, I wanted to improve upon this model so I decided to use some of the four factors with additional parameters. Recently, pace of play has become an emphasis in the NBA. When I tried to use this in my, "mymodel" predictor, I found that the pace had little impact (low coefficient value) so I ended up experimenting with other parameters. I eventually found that a team's defensive rating gave much better accuracy in predictions. I also used TS% instead of eFG% which takes into account three point field goal percentage and free throws. Based upon these changes I was able to improve my model score from 0.9351(Four Factor Model) to 0.9775(My Model).
